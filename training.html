Security isn't a dead-end job — it's a ladder. Here's the climb, and the training that unlocks each rung.

Licensed Officer

Guard Card + fingerprints. Working unarmed posts within weeks — retail, corporate lobbies, events.

Start earning now

Certified Specialist

Add Triple Cert, MOAB, CPR. Qualify for hands-on posts — hospitals, schools, DCF facilities — at higher rates.

Armed Officer
