How this works

Three steps. No fluff, no semesters.

Other careers want four years and your savings. This one wants a Saturday and a clean record.

01

Sit the class

Eight hours, in person, with Lou. You walk out the same day holding a folder with everything: your certificate, the state forms signed, directions to DESPP, and the employer hiring list Lou keeps himself.

02

File with the state

Take the folder to Middletown, get printed, done. The state issues the card — Lou makes sure your paperwork is right before you ever leave the room. No bounced applications, no second trips.

03

Go to work

Security companies hire straight out of this class — some graduates are on a post inside two weeks. Hundreds of CT openings on the free job board, updated daily. Like the man says: the rest is up to you.
